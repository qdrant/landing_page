# Optimization

Qdrant is designed to be tunable — you can trade off memory usage, search speed, and precision depending on your workload. This section covers strategies for getting the most out of your deployment.

## Guides

- [Optimize Performance](/documentation/optimization/optimize/) — Walk through three key optimization scenarios: high-speed search with low memory, high precision with low memory, and high precision with high speed.
- [Optimizer](/documentation/optimization/optimizer/) — Understand how Qdrant's background optimizer rebuilds segments to reclaim memory, maintain index quality, and keep search performance consistent over time.
